Welcome to the personal website of Francis Effah Boateng

I am a graduate student at the University of Maine School of Economics.

My research interests lie in econometric connectedness and market integration, exploring how various economic variables and markets interact and influence each other to better understand global financial dynamics. I am particularly interested in my macroeconomics, econometrics, and financial economics research. I am currently a member of the Market Integration Research Lab (MIRL) at UMaine.

At the undergraduate level, I have taught courses in Elementary Statistical methods, Design of experiments, Non-Parametric Statistics, Urban Economics, and Principles of Macroeconomics.

I am currently in my final year in the Master of Science in Economics at the University of Maine and my advisors are Thomas Wiesen and Todd Gabe . In 2022, I earned my Bachelor of Arts Cum Laude in Statistics and Economics from the University of Ghana where I was taught and advised by Eric Nyarko. mmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmm
